## Ground rule

Everything here is for **defending systems you own or are authorized to test**. Skills are practiced on purpose-built labs (TryHackMe, HackTheBox, OWASP Juice Shop) — never on real systems without written authorization. That's not just ethics, it's the law.

## Mindset

> An attacker needs one way in. A defender needs to know all of them.

Security is not a product you install; it's a property of how you build, configure and operate everything else. That's why this section lives next to [devsecops](../devsecops/).
